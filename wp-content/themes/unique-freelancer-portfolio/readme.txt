== Description ==

The Unique Freelancer Portfolio is free and specially crafted considering the unique needs of freelancers who want to showcase their work professionally. It offers a sleek and modern design which can be beneficial for tough competition amongst competitors. The theme is most suitable for writers, photographers, designers, Portfolio projects, Portfolio presentation, Portfolio site, Portfolio management, Portfolio branding, Portfolio UI, and Portfolio UX, Portfolio content, Portfolio multimedia, Portfolio work samples, Portfolio documentation,and Portfolio statement.. The Homepage offers an excellent header section that includes an announcement Bar, a Horizontal Menu Bar, and an attractive banner to represent your core skills to potential clients. A special section to showcase past projects with short descriptions helps in building credibility and trust among clients. Further contact form plays an important role in communicating with clients and our theme leaves no stone unturned to do so, offering dedicated and highly functional contact forms that even grab data of customers. Additionally, Responsive design and SEO practices ensure easy access of the website on any type of device and website ranks on Google search engines.

== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the Add New button.
2. Click Upload and Choose File, then select the theme's .zip file. Click Install Now.
3. Click Activate to use your Unique Freelancer Portfolio right away.
